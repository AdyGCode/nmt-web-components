To make it easier to plan your studies we provide a calendar that spans the current year (from 2023 onwards) plus, when ready, we also will publish upcoming years.

A planner is also provided to assist you planning the study and assessment completion across multiple clusters.

Blackboard Collaborate

Blackboard Collaborate is the interactive collaboration system that we use to provide recorded sessions and support.

Important: Lectures are not recorded unless a lecturer is presenting remotely. All in class lecturers are not recorded under most situations.
